# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or level of experience.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, respectful, and assume good intent
- Give clear, actionable feedback focused on the work
- Respect different perspectives and experience levels
- Focus on improving the project collaboratively

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, edit or remove content, and restrict participation to keep the project healthy and productive.

Issues can be reported to [conduct@euraika.net](mailto:conduct@euraika.net). All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
